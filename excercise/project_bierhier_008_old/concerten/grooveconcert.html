Groove Control

 Groove Control believes in the fact that house music unites by the common devotion and spirit for warm grooves. Bringing you estabalished names, accompanied by emerging talents from Amsterdam’s local scene. Sharing our boundless admiration for music that almost sounds transcendental. A weekly night that cherishes the past and welcomes the present.
